Frequently Asked Questions - General

How do I search the FAQ site?

Use the keyboard shortcut Control/Command + F and use the search function in your browser to find the FAQ you need.

Download the PDF for iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) here.

Download the PDF for Android devices here.

What if I forgot my password?

Press the Forgot Password link at the login page of the comCOACHgo app. You will receive an email with a temporary password so you can log in and change your password. The email will be from comCOACHgo@success-sciences.com. Please check your spam if you don't see if in your inbox.

What devices can comCOACHgo run on?

comCOACHgo is compatible with iOS devices (iPhones and iPads) with iOS 9.1 or later and Android devices with Android 5.0 and up. comCOACHgo is currently not compatible with Windows devices or Kindle devices.

Can users submit more than one response per challenge?

Users can only submit one video response per challenge. The video cannot be deleted unless you or the company admin deletes the challenge.
